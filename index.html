About Klinam

Turning waste overflow into a dependable neighborhood utility

Klinam is designed for university-adjacent residential zones where sanitation challenges are persistent and conventional municipal services are inefficient. The platform combines community reporting, operations dispatch, and sustainable material recovery into one practical service model.

Smart Reporting

Caretakers report full bins through a simple interface, triggering immediate dispatch prioritization.

Flexible Operations

Small, agile utility vehicles navigate difficult roads and service locations that larger trucks cannot access.

Circular Recovery

Collected materials are sorted and routed into recyclable plastics and organic waste streams for downstream value.
