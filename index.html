The Bottleneck

The Physical Reality of Off-Campus Terrains

Traditional municipal compactor trucks completely fail in student residential corridors. Narrow pathways, unpaved terrain, and rugged roads lock out heavy government sanitation infrastructure, leaving local areas completely isolated from standard disposal services.

The Consequence

The Massive Cost of Environmental Inaction

When utility trucks can't access homes, trash piles up at the doorstep. This leads to severe public health risks, localized rodent infestations, and open trash accumulation that blocks drainage channels—accelerating devastating road erosion during the rainy season.

The Architecture

Our Agile Fleet & Fintech Solution

Klinam replaces massive municipal trucks with an agile fleet of highly maneuverable, small-scale vehicles. We eliminate cash transaction overhead entirely by automating collections through the OPay Recurrent Billing API, tracking property states via a lightweight caretaker reporting portal.
